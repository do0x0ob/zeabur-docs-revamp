# Deploy from Template

> **Suggested CTA placement:** Template marketplace header; empty project page → Deploy new service → Deploy from template

No code needed. Pick a template, follow the template’s instructions, and you’re ready to experience self‑hosted services.

**Prerequisite:** You need a [dedicated server](https://zeabur.com/docs/en-US/dedicated-server). If you don’t have one yet, you can rent a new server from Zeabur during the flow and experience self‑hosted services. For pricing, see [Choose your cloud, we handle the rest with AI](https://zeabur.com/en-US/pricing).

---

## Pick a Template

The **Template Marketplace** is Zeabur’s preset template library, with templates maintained by Zeabur or certified partners — blogs, automation, databases, monitoring, AI apps, and more. Pick a template, fill in the deployment settings, and deploy with one click. No need to write code or set up the environment yourself.

Go to the [Template Marketplace](https://zeabur.com/en-US/templates), or click **Deploy new service → Template** in your project.

Not sure which to pick? Here are two to get started:

| Template | Use case |
|----------|----------|
| [WordPress](https://zeabur.com/en-US/templates/CV344X) | Sites and blogs — open it and see something right away |
| [SillyTavern](https://zeabur.com/en-US/templates/DX09NM) | AI character chat and storytelling UI, ready to use out of the box |

## Deploy

1. On the template page, click **Deploy** to open that template’s deployment settings.
2. Enter the initial config and choose the target server (or let the project decide).
3. When deployment finishes, you’ll be redirected to the service page.

There is no step four.

## Open Your Service

When the **Domain** on the service page shows **PROVISIONED**, click the link to access your service.

Note: Depending on the template, some services may require a key or password to access. Check the template’s docs and the service page for the values Zeabur generated for you — and don’t share those secrets. If you haven’t set a domain yet, the easiest way to access is the **Networking** tab: bind a Zeabur subdomain to reach the service.

---

## Want to Use Your Own Domain?

On the service’s **Networking** page, click **Bind custom domain**, then point your DNS to the record Zeabur gives you. After a few minutes you can use your custom domain. See [Public networking](https://zeabur.com/docs/en-US/networking/public) for details.
